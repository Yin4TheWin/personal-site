Bot Ink

Bot Ink is a service which allows its users to create custom Discord bots for their servers. Each bot has a music player, moderation features and an extensive support ticket system.

Twinhat

A portmanteau of "two minute chat", Twinhat is a service that makes it easy for you to create a public chatroom. Simply sign up on the website, enter the name of your desired chatroom, and hit create! Perfect for businesses that want a quick customer service solution and friends who want to make a quick chat room with no sign up required to chat.

Fantasy Fitness Quest

This project won best Health Hack at HackRU 22! Fantasy Fitness Quest is an app that aims to make taking care of yourself fun by turning exercise and healthy eating into an RPG game. Connect your Google Fit account and input what you eat daily to make your character hit harder and survive longer. Then, use your newfound strength to vanquish your foes in the Battle screen!
